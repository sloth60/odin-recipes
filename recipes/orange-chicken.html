Orange Chicken

Prep time: 15 Minutes | Cook time: 20 Minutes

Ingredients

  • Aidell's Chicken Sausages
  • 4 Bell Peppers(Red, Yellow, Orange, Green)
  • 1 Onion
  • Olive Oil (2 tablespoons)
  • Salt and Pepper (to taste)
  • 8 x 10 Casserole Dish

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees
  2. Open Chicken Sausage and Cut into 1/2 to 3/4 inch chunks, add to pan
  3. Julienne Peppers and add to pan
  4. Mix ingredients thoroughly
  5. Pour olive oil over mixture and continue stirring
  6. Add Salt and Pepper to taste
  7. Place in oven for ~20 minutes, or until peppers are soft and sausage is cooked through
  8. Enjoy!
