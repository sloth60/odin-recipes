Pork Loin

Home
Prep time: 10 Minutes | Cook time: 120 Minutes

The easiest and tastiest way to make grilled pork loin!

Ingredients

  • 4 lb Pork Loin
  • 1 Tbsp Garlic Powder
  • 1 Tbsp Onion Powder
  • 1 Tbsp Smoked Paprika
  • 1 Tbsp Dry Mustard
  • 2 Tbsp Seasoned Salt
  • 1/2 Tbsp Ginger
  • Mustard (5 tablespoons)
  • Olive Oil
  • 1 Tbsp Salt and Pepper (each)
  • 8 x 10 Grill safe Casserole Dish

Directions

  1. Preheat Grill to 425 Degrees F
  2. Oil Bottom of pan using olive oil
  3. Place loin in dish, fat side up
  4. Mix spices in separate bowl or container
  5. Coat loin in mustard (this will not add much flavor, just help create sticky surface for spices)
  6. Cover loin in seasoning and rub in
  7. Turn off burners of 1/2 of the grill
  8. Place loin over burners that are off
  9. Allow Loin to convection cook for up to 2 hours, or until reaching an internal temperature of 145
  10. If you need to cook it slightly faster, you can turn on the burners beneath the loin to the lowest setting
  11. Allow meat to rest for 5-10 minutes, then slice into 1/2 inch pieces to serve
  12. Enjoy!
